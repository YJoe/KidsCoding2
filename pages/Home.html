What we do

Kids coding provides children with the ability to be a part of an exciting and fast growing industry. Based in Reading, possibly the best location in the country for technology related careers, we hope to open up channels for young people in the area to communicate with these businesses and maybe even secure jobs for them in future. If we can gain enough momentum, we may be able to set up workshops with these firms which will give the students opportunities to see how the skills they have been honing can be applied to the real world.
