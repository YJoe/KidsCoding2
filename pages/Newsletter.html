New Location

This past week, due to all of your funding and interest in our courses (as well as our bake sale being a huge success!) we have managed to secure another location at St Josephs College to hold more of our weekly sessions, bringing us up to a total of six locations. This will allow us to take on more kids and get them creating their own apps in no time! Again we would like to thank all of our donors as well as all of the volunteers who have given their time to help us grow. - James Coleby 12/03/2014

Bake Sale Success

Our annual bake sale has gone down a treat yet again, with a turnout of more than 30 families from our sessions ringing in a grand total of £821! This money will go towards all of the resources necessary to keep our classes going. Special thanks to our tp fundraiser Tyrone Jeromson who managed to raise £74.60! - Emily Parker 21/07/2015

Awards Winners!

Last week brought us the coveted KidsCoding excellence awards ceremony, which showcases our best and brightest volunteers and students and recognises outstanding achievement. Our big winner was Rodney Danton who took home the awards for best volunteer, best volunteer lesson plan and the award for perfect attendance. Our winner of most improved student was Sarah Jane Tonna, who managed to create a fully functioning game of Pong on a Raspberry Pi from having no prior experience in programming! Other winners include Jeff Winger for best program in Java, Terrence Smith for best program in C, and Terry Jones for winning our Hackathon. - Emily Parker 17/02/2016
