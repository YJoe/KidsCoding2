New Location

This past week, due to all of your funding and interest in our courses (as well as our bake sale being a huge success!) we have managed to secure another location at Reading School to hold more of our weekly sessions, bringing us up to a total of six locations. This will allow us to take on more kids and get them creating their own apps in no time! Again we would like to thank all of our donors as well as all of the volunteers who have given their time to help us grow. - Tom 33

Bake Sale Success

Our annual bake sale has gone down a treat yet again, with a turnout of more than 30 families from our sessions bringing in a grand total of £821! This money will go towards all of the resources necessary to keep our classes going. Special thanks to our tp fundraiser Tyrone Jeromson who managed to raise £74.60! - Tom 33
