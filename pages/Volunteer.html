To be a volunteer you need programming experience in any language and an enthusiasm to teach young minds. You would also need to be able to dedicate regular amounts of time to running a session,l once a week for a term(12 weeks). The responsibilities include preparing content and resources each week.

Fill in the form below with your personal details, upload a CV detailing any programming experience or past experience dealing with children. Upload a valid DBS check and Kids Coding will get in touch.
