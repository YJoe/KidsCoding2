Why volunteer?


Help shape a child’s future.

Get involved with this amazing course expand your own skill set and teach children of varying ages to program in languages such as Pascal and Python. Some other skills you may gain are planning a curriculum, managing a class and effective communication, as you will have to be able to explain how the program works at a level such that those with no experience can clearly understand what’s going on.

The opportunities we offer could be invaluable for you if you wish to undertake technologically related career path. We encourage people of all ages with any kind of technological background from student to CEO of your own firm to apply. To find your nearest club, click here.

To be a volunteer you need programming experience in any language and an enthusiasm to teach young minds. You would also need to be able to dedicate regular amounts of time to running a session, once a week for a term (12 weeks). The responsibilities include preparing content and resources each week.

Fill in the form below with your personal details, upload a CV detailing any programming experience or past experience dealing with children. Upload a valid DBS check and Kids Coding will get in touch.
