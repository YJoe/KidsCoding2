What we do

Kids Coding is a charity that was set up in early 2014 ins central Reading and aims to provide local children (aged 9-14) with free access to after- school clubs where they can learn the foundations of computer programming.

These clubs are run by our enthusiastic volunteers and managed by our employees who encourage the kids to aim high and be creative, as well as providing them with a new, sought after skill at an early age the sessions improve the children’s confidence and give them a greater connection with an ever changing world, and potentially a new, useful hobby.

What we do

Kids Coding run their ‘coding clubs’ at local schools. The schools provide the resources which are key to the children's learning. To sign up your school click here.

As Kids Coding rely on the generous donations from people like you to be able to run the sessions and to be able to bring in inspirational people from within to computer science industry. The money also helps to provide extra resources such as programmable robots which give a hands on approach to programming which allows the children to have fun as well as learning. Any donation ,even a small contribution, can help build a child’s future.

We run regular fundraising events organised and supervised by our brilliant volunteers and students. These events include raffles with amazing prizes or our more recent bake sale within the local community which can be seen in our newsletter.
